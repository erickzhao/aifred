Depression is a serious mental illness that globally affects more than 300 million people over the course their lives1. Some patients can spend years finding the right treatment regimen for them, and many patients won’t improve after the first treatment2. The inability to predict any given individual’s unique response to medical treatment is arguably one of the biggest bottlenecks to mental health recovery.

This is where aifred comes in. The aifred solution will be a clinical software tool acting as a trusted advisor for healthcare professionals to optimize treatment quality and efficacy for mental health. This tool is a comprehensive, evidence-based decision aid for healthcare professionals to assist in rapidly selecting the most effective treatments for any given patient, as early on in their clinical course as possible, in order to reduce time to remission of symptoms as much as possible while minimizing burdensome side effects. Initially, we will be focusing on treatments for depression, but we plan to scale aifred to encompass all mental health conditions in order to amplify clinical utility. At its core, aifred is leveraging the collective intelligence of the scientific and medical community to bring better healthcare to all.

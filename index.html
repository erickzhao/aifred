Depression is a common, serious mental illness, that affects more than 300 million people globally 1. Some patients can spend years finding the right choice for them, and many patients won’t improve after the first treatment 2. The inability to predict any given individual’s heterogeneous response to medical treatment is arguably one of the biggest bottlenecks to mental health recovery. The aifred solution will be a tool acting as a trusted advisor for healthcare professionals to optimize treatment quality and efficacy in psychiatry. Initially, we will be focusing on treatments for depression, but we plan to scale this tool to encompass all mental health conditions in order to amplify clinical utility. At its core, aifred is leveraging the collective intelligence of the scientific and medical community to bring better healthcare to everyone.

X
Aifred acknowledges patient diversity and brings highly tailored treatments to each individual, creating an avenue for doctors to apply personalized, evidence-based medicine in its most rigorous form. Aifred will learn to predict the optimal treatment cocktail for any given patient, while minimizing adverse effects and filtering out safety concerns such drug-disease or drug-drug interactions. This in turn will lead to faster recovery or/and remission, while reducing the potential dangers that arise due to delayed treatment response, such as suicide. With better treatment effectiveness and side-effect prediction, personalized treatments can be chosen faster, saving on costs, limiting suffering, and empowering physicians and their patients.

The aifred solution is a clinical decision aid system built with machine learning techniques, aimed at helping clinicians choose the most effective treatments for mental health problems, given individual patient characteristics, in order to reduce time to remission of symptoms as much as possible while minimizing adverse side effects. By training our model on large datasets, which include information about socio-demographic factors, symptom profiles, as well as genetic, metabolic, endocrine, immune, and neuroimaging data, aifred will learn to predict the optimal treatment cocktail for any given patient, with the patient outcomes feeding back into aifred’s database to improve its predictive power.

For a patient with depression, trying a new treatment means several weeks of therapy or medication titration to start seeing if there is a positive effect. This is time lost in the patient’s life - time that is potentially away from work and time when they are not able to be fully present in their families’ lives. This is where aifred comes in. The aifred solution is comprehensive, evidence-based tool for healthcare professionals to assist in rapidly selecting the most effective treatments for any given patient, as early on in their clinical course as possible, in order to reduce time to remission of symptoms as much as possible while minimizing burdensome side effects.

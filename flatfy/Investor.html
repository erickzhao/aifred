Relevance

Mental health is a constant topic of conversation and for good reason; according to Statistics Canada, 1 in 5 Canadians will experience a mental illness at some point in their lifetime. With this massive prevalence, aifred addresses a huge need in society: taking action to improve mental health treatment.

Innovation

This is a critical time in which artificial intelligence provides uncontested potential to integrate data analysis and healthcare, creating the ultimate network to fight mental illness based on the work of doctors, researchers and patients themselves. By forming connections between diverse experts in the field of mental health, aifred itself has innate long term relevancy and answers the call for action in addressing the issue of mental health treatment.

Potential

Aifred does this scientifically, using artificial neural network models of machine learning to predict treatment efficacy based on historic and current research data. As a valuable resource for medical professionals, aifred has potential to be a fundamental step forward in an area desperately in need of innovation, with massive implications for future development and refinement as well.

Clinical Decision Aid for Increased Treatment Efficacy

Personalization

In order to optimize the treatments received by people battling mental illness, aifred uses biological data from current mental health research to predict the best treatment method for individual patients based on their personal health information.

Efficiency

In this way, aifred addresses the complexities of patients with mental illness and bases its predictions off of up-to-date scientific findings to reduce negative side effects, minimize financial costs on patients, and accelerate the process of finding the proper treatment specific to a patientâ€™s biological needs.

Application

aifred is a resource for medical professionals who want to take advantage of the massive amounts of research in the field of mental health and harness this data for improving medical treatment efficacy. By constantly refining and improving itself based on the work of the people who fight the battle against mental illness (researchers, doctors and patients), aifred breaks down the complexities and intricacies of mental health data to build updated treatment advice.
